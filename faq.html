When is the best time to install sod? ▼

Sod can be installed most times of year, but spring and fall offer the best results. These seasons provide cooler temperatures and adequate moisture for establishment. Summer installations require extra watering, while winter is generally not recommended in most climates.

How long do retaining walls last? ▼

A properly built retaining wall can last 30-50+ years depending on materials and maintenance. Stone and concrete walls tend to last longer than timber. Our walls are built with proper drainage and engineering to maximize longevity.
